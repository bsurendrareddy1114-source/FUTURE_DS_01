# Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes business sales performance across regions, product categories, and time periods. The dashboard provides insights into revenue trends, profit performance, regional contributions, and top-performing products to support data-driven business decisions.

## Objectives

* Analyze total sales, profit, and order performance.
* Identify top-performing regions and product categories.
* Track revenue trends over time.
* Discover top-selling products based on revenue.
* Support strategic business decision-making through data visualization.

## Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards (Total Orders, Total Revenue, Profit Margin %, Total Profit)
* Revenue Trend Analysis by Year
* Revenue by Region
* Revenue by Category
* Top 10 Products by Revenue
* Regional Revenue Comparison
* Interactive Filters for Dynamic Analysis

## Key Insights

1. The business generated **$2.30M** in total revenue with **$286.40K** in total profit.
2. Technology is the highest-performing category, followed by Furniture and Office Supplies.
3. The West region contributes the highest revenue, while the South region contributes the lowest.
4. Several products consistently generate strong revenue and contribute significantly to overall sales.
5. Revenue demonstrates an increasing trend over the analyzed period.

## Recommendations

1. Expand investment in the Technology category to maximize revenue growth.
2. Increase marketing efforts in lower-performing regions, especially the South.
3. Maintain inventory levels for top-performing products.
4. Replicate successful sales strategies from high-performing regions.
5. Continuously monitor revenue trends to support forecasting and planning.

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel / CSV Dataset
* DAX Measures
* Data Modeling
* Data Visualization
